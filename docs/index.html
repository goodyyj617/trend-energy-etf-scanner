Metric Definitions and Active Gate Thresholds

Signal inputs

R20: Close-to-close 20-trading-day return.

ER20: Upside net movement over 20 days divided by the sum of absolute daily close changes; negative net movement is clipped to zero.

TE63 / TE126: 63- or 126-day return multiplied by its matching upside efficiency ratio.

Score: 0.65 × TE63 + 0.35 × TE126.

Score Breakout: Score exceeds its prior score_lookback high, Score is positive, R20/ER20 pass their minimums, and Close is above MA50.

score_lookback: Number of prior trading days used for the Score high comparison.

Entry and exit rules

First signal: Next-open entry after the signal first changes from false to true.

Signal 2D / 3D confirm: Next-open entry after two or three consecutive true signal days.

5D breakout after signal: Within five days of the first signal, Close must break the prior five-day high; entry is at the next open.

Low10 / Low20 trailing: Signal-day rolling low is the initial stop and the stop can only move upward.

Low20 - 0.5ATR trailing: Low20 minus half ATR20, trailed upward only.

Chandelier20 2.5ATR: HHV20 minus 2.5 × ATR20, trailed upward only.

MA50 trailing: MA50 price stop, trailed upward only.

Max holding days: Exit at the close after 63 trading days if no stop exits first.

Round-trip cost: 0.2% is deducted once from each completed trade's gross return. A gap below the stop exits at the open; otherwise a touched stop exits at the stop.

Completed-trade results

Completed Trades: Exit-applied trades with realized outcomes.

Avg / Median Trade Ret: Mean or median completed net trade return. Median Trade Ret is diagnostic and is not a qualification condition.

Trade Win Rate: Share of completed net trade returns above zero.

Sum of Trade Returns: Arithmetic sum of completed net trade returns; not portfolio return.

Trade-Sequence DD: Drawdown of the entry-date-ordered compounded trade sequence; not portfolio drawdown.

Profit Factor: Positive return sum divided by the absolute negative return sum.

Worst / 10th Pctl Trade Ret: Worst and lower-tail completed net trade outcomes.

Stop Hit Rate: Share of completed trades exited by a price stop.

Avg Days: Mean trading-day holding length.

Eligible entry year: A full calendar entry year with at least 100 completed trades. Partial years remain visible but are excluded from the Time Gate.

Annual joint-positive year: An eligible year with Avg Trade Ret above zero and Profit Factor above 1.0. Annual median return is diagnostic only.

Joint-positive-year ratio: Joint-positive eligible years divided by eligible full entry years.

LOYO fold: One eligible year is omitted and pooled completed trades, arithmetic sum returns, gross profit, and gross loss are reconstructed from the remaining annual aggregates.

LOYO pass ratio: Passing LOYO folds divided by eligible folds; each fold needs at least 100 trades, positive average return, and Profit Factor at least 1.2.

Effective neighbor: A direct adjacent Score parameter neighbor after candidate-identical outcomes are removed and duplicate aggregate-plus-annual outcome signatures are counted once.

Effective neighbor edge pass ratio: Effective eligible neighbors passing the independent overall Sample and Edge conditions divided by effective eligible neighbors.

Qualification Tier: Qualified only when Sample, Edge, Time, and effective Parameter gates all pass.

Bootstrap: Diagnostic only; it does not affect production qualification or ranking.

Limit: These are provisional in-sample robustness gates. Qualification does not establish out-of-sample profitability.
